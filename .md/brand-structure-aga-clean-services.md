# AGA Clean Services Brand Structure

Source: `BRAND STRUCTURE ⭐ AGA Clean Services.docx`

## Brand

- Business name: AGA Clean Services
- Service area: Newcastle, Gateshead, Durham
- Positioning: reliable, friendly, flexible, affordable cleaning service
- Core value: clients buy trust, peace of mind and consistency, not only cleaning

## Priority channels

### 1. Facebook

Target:

- Newcastle groups
- Gateshead community groups
- Polish groups in the UK
- Buy & Sell groups
- Local ads groups

Ready post:

```text
✨ AGA Clean Services ✨

Professional Home & Commercial Cleaning
Newcastle • Gateshead • Durham

✔ Regular home cleaning
✔ Deep cleaning
✔ End of tenancy
✔ Offices & small businesses

✅ Reliable & friendly service
✅ Flexible hours
✅ Affordable prices

📩 Message now for free quote!
📱 WhatsApp: 07xxxxx
```

Add:

- logo or service photo
- before / after photos later

### 2. Gumtree / Facebook Marketplace

- Title: `Cleaning Services Newcastle | AGA Clean Services`
- Category: `Services -> Cleaning`
- Description: use the same ad copy as Facebook

### 3. Google Business Profile

Create profile:

- `AGA Clean Services`

Add:

- logo
- description
- phone number

After launch:

- collect reviews from first clients

### 4. Flyers

Distribute:

- under doors
- on housing estates
- in Polish shops

Short flyer text:

```text
Cleaning Services - AGA Clean Services
Domestic • Commercial • Airbnb
Call or WhatsApp: 07xxxxx
```

### 5. Letting agents and Airbnb hosts

Target:

- letting agencies
- Airbnb hosts

Pitch:

```text
I do end of tenancy cleaning.
```

This is one of the strongest higher-value service lines.
