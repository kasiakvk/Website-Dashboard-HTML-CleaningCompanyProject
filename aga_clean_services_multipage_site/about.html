Growth Plan

Launch channels connected into one simple acquisition system.

1. Facebook first

Priority channel for local visibility and early enquiries.

  • Newcastle and Gateshead community groups
  • Polish groups in the UK
  • Buy & sell and local ads groups

2. Gumtree and Marketplace

Repeat the same core offer with local service keywords.

  • Title: Cleaning Services Newcastle | AGA Clean Services
  • Category: Services → Cleaning
  • Use the same short sales copy

3. Google Business Profile

Essential for search trust and review collection.

  • Business name, logo and description
  • Phone and WhatsApp contact
  • Ask early clients for reviews immediately

4. Offline flyers

Support online channels with local physical reach.

  • Door drops on residential estates
  • Polish shops and community spaces
  • Simple domestic and commercial service message

5. Letting agents and Airbnb hosts

Higher-value outreach focused on tenancy and turnover cleaning.

  • Pitch end-of-tenancy cleaning directly
  • Message 5 to 10 agents in days 4 to 7
  • Target repeat work, not one-off leads only

Execution rhythm

Short launch sequence for the first two weeks.

  • Days 1-3: Facebook posts, Gumtree, Google profile
  • Days 4-7: direct outreach to agents and hosts
  • Week 2: first clients and review collection
