# AGA Clean Services Brand Guide

## Positioning

AGA Clean Services is a local cleaning brand for Newcastle, Gateshead and Durham focused on domestic, commercial and landlord-facing work.

## Core promise

Clients are not only buying cleaning. They are buying:

- Trust
- Peace of mind
- Reliability
- Someone who does not let them down

## Tone of voice

- Clear
- Friendly
- Reliable
- Practical
- Premium without sounding corporate

## Key offer lines

- Professional Home & Commercial Cleaning
- Reliable and friendly service
- Flexible hours
- Affordable prices
- Free quote by message or WhatsApp

## Priority audiences

- Homeowners and tenants
- Letting agencies
- Airbnb hosts
- Offices and small businesses
- Shops and salons
- Clubs and pubs needing after-hours cleaning

## Coverage area

- Newcastle
- Gateshead
- Durham
