# Launch Plan

## Priority channel order

1. Facebook
2. Gumtree and Facebook Marketplace
3. Google Business Profile
4. Flyers
5. Letting agents and Airbnb hosts

## Facebook groups to target

- Newcastle groups
- Gateshead community groups
- Polish groups in the UK
- Buy and Sell groups
- Local ads groups

## Google Business setup

Create a profile for `AGA Clean Services` and add:

- Logo
- Business description
- Phone number
- WhatsApp contact

Collect reviews as early as possible after the first jobs.

## Offline flyer plan

Distribute flyers:

- Under doors
- On local estates
- In Polish shops

## Agent outreach

Use a direct pitch:

`I do end-of-tenancy cleaning.`

This is the strongest route into higher-value landlord and letting work.

## First 14 days

### Days 1-3

- Facebook posts
- Gumtree listing
- Google profile setup

### Days 4-7

- Message 5-10 agents or Airbnb hosts

### Week 2

- Convert early enquiries
- Deliver first jobs
- Ask for reviews immediately
