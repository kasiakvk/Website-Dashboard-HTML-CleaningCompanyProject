Meet Agnieszka - Your Local Cleaning Professional

Agnieszka Kalina vel Kalinowska is the founder and owner of AGU Clean Services. With years of cleaning and client-service experience, she has built the business around reliability, quality and genuine care for every job.

My Work

I approach every project individually, taking time to understand each client's needs and tailoring the service so the final result matches their expectations.

I focus on reliability, punctuality and high-quality service. I use proven products and safe cleaning methods that help keep spaces fresh and clean for longer.

Experience

Over the years, I have gained experience in both private homes and commercial environments. That experience has taught me the value of care, precision and responsibility in every stage of the job.

  • Completing every stage of the job with care and precision
  • Respecting each client's property and personal space
  • Maintaining discretion and professionalism at all times
  • Working efficiently, effectively and with full responsibility

Reliability and Trust

Clients choose AGU Clean Services because they want someone dependable, careful and easy to communicate with from the first message to the finished clean.

What Clients Value Most

  • Reliability and honesty
  • Attention to detail
  • Consistently high standards of service
  • Friendly communication and a professional approach

Inviting someone into your home or business is an important decision. That is why I always work to make every professional relationship comfortable, transparent and built on mutual trust.
