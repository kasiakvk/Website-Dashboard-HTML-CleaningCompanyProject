Privacy policy

This page explains how AGU CLEANING SERVICES handles website enquiries and basic client contact details.

Who we are

AGA Clean Services provides domestic, tenancy, Airbnb and commercial cleaning services in Newcastle, Gateshead, Chester-le-Street and Durham.

What information we collect

When you use the contact form or request a quote, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, service request and property details.

How we use your information

Your information is used only to reply to enquiries, prepare quotes, arrange bookings and manage client communication.

Data storage

Website enquiry data is stored in the project backend for lead management. We do not use your details for unrelated marketing.

Data sharing

Your data is not sold. Information is only shared where reasonably necessary to provide the requested cleaning service or comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your enquiry data by contacting agucleanservices@gmail.com.
